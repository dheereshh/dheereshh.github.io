Mental Health

We are here to help, you can always talk to us

We work across our communities in London to advocate for mental health awareness. It is important to look after our mental health as it affects how we think and feels about ourselves and the world around us. Mental health problems can feel overwhelming and can impact our everyday life. It might make a person feel numb, unable to cope, disconnected from family and friends and less hopeful about the future. We always strive to support anyone who may feel any of these emotions. Everyone with a mental health problem has the right to access the care and services they need, and we believe you should be treated fairly, positively and with respect.

There are many different types of mental health problems that are common including anxiety, depression, eating disorders, self-harm, bipolar disorder and many more.

If your mental or emotional state quickly gets worse, or you’re worried about someone you know – help is available. You’re not alone; talk to someone you trust. Sharing a problem is often the first step to recovery. Please call us on +44 7747302134 or message us in here for Mental Health Support.

If you are or someone you know is struggling with any mental health issues, then you do not have to go through that alone.

Our team at MACWO can help you find the right support that is suitable for you. Regardless of what is on your mind, you can always talk to us.
